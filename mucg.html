ABOUT

Founded in 2000, Methodist University College is a private higher education institution located in the urban setting of the large city of Accra (population range of 1,000,000-5,000,000 inhabitants), Greater Accra. Officially accredited and/or recognized by the National Accreditation Board, Ghana (National Accreditation Board, Ghana), Methodist University College (MUCG) is a very small (uniRank enrollment range: 1,000-1,999 students) coeducational higher education institution formally affiliated with the Christian-Methodist religion. Methodist University College (MUCG) offers courses and programs leading to officially recognized higher education degrees such as bachelor degrees in several areas of study

schedule of fees

The following must be observed:
1. Fresh students are required to pay the full fees before registration.
2. Continuing students :
(i) 60% of fees must be paid by the end of the fourth (4th) week of the semester/trimester.
(ii) 80% of fees must be paid by the end of the sixth (6th) week of the semester/trimester.
(iii) 100% payment must be made before the commencement of the end-of- semester/trimester examinations.
NOTE: No fee defaulter will be permitted to write mid-semester/mid-trimester, and end-of- semester/trimester examinations.
